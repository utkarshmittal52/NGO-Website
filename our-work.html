Our Work

01.

Teaching

  • With the help of our team, we try to reach every kid who wants to study and has the will to change their future. We provide both online and offline means of education
  • In offline education, we manage a team to open a camp nearby our kid's locations and, there our team distribute children in several groups, which were taught by different individuals on daily basis on different days different people cover all the subjects.
  • In online education, we first ensure that the kid has the appropriate means through which they can attend the class and then follow the same process as offline.

02.

Cloth Donation

  • We conduct regular cloth donation drives at the individual and team levels. We try to carry out door-to-door collection drives to collect as many clothes as we can.
  • Once, done with collecting, we plan to meet and pile up clothes according to the several sizes.
  • Lastly, our team plans and donates these clothes to the families of our children and others in need.

03.

Food Donation

  • We try and collaborate with other NGOs or societies who are working towards feeding the poor, and ensure that none of our kids or their family members sleep on an empty stomach.
  • Other than this, we from our donations, provide rations to the family in their hard times
  • We know the value of food and how much its important for the development of children, so ensure that they have sufficient to eat.

04.

Health Camps

  • Our members connect with the medical teams or doctors who are interested in providing a free health check-up for our kids.
  • We try to at least conduct a health camp twice a year.
  • We do a collaborative effort to ensure the good health of our kids as a healthy and sound body is very important for their growth

05.

Sanitary Donation

  • Through this we are looking after Menstrual Hygiene of our children falling under the age group & also of females of their family.
  • We try to collect the sanitary pads from wherever and whom so ever possible.
  • We are strongly committed to bring major change in life of many of girls through this initiative.

06.

Appreciation Program

  • In this initative we appreciate and award those students who are doing exceptionally well in respective fields.
  • Our motive is to motivate these students and provide them a ray of hope that they can definitely do better tomorrow if they work hard today.

07.

Sports Fest

  • We celebrate a day full of happiness and enjoyment
  • We organise several sports event for our kids.The events are arranged according to the different age groups.
  • We look forward to bring joy in lives of our kids and to teach them that overall development is important.

08.

Festivals

  • We try and celebrate all the festivals with the kids, as it helps them understand their culture better
  • It helps them understand the story behind every festival and its motive.
