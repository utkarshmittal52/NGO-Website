Become A sponsor

You can help a child by sponsoring his/ her school fee. You can become a distant parent of a needy child. We provide you with a monthly update of your sponsored child and about his/her academic performance. If you need you can also stay in direct contact with the child and the parents.

What you have to do to become a sponsor?

You have to adopt a child by sponsoring his expenses or become his caretaker.

You have to pay the kids school fee which will in between 10,000 to 12,500 rupees anually, depending upon in which class the kid study.

You have to arrange the books for the kids education.

We will be giving you regular report about the performance of your sponsored kid.

We can arrange meetings for you with your adopted kid according to your will.
