Together

Ubharta Bharat is an initiative to help underprivileged children across India. Our NGO aims to educate these children and to fulfill their basic needs which will include school fees, stationery, food, and clothes. We are initiating this with a dream of educating India’s youth to make India a better India.

Our Mission

We aspire to see an educated India. We wish that every child has the right to education and, we are here to help those who themselves can not afford it. Our main motive is to educate the youth of India so they can make their future better and live the life they want to.

Get to know us better

Who are we?

We are a group of socially active, determined & educated youths, aims to educate underprivileged children. We are here with a thought: “Why only we? Why not the children living in slums have an access to quality education?” Neither we nor they chose the families we are born and also there is no such right that bestows the blessing of education upon us… only us. Every time we think of these children, a question arises in our mind- “What if all these facilities & luxury, which we are enjoying, weren’t available to us?”

Our Team
